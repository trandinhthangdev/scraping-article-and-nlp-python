Liverpool beat Flamengo with an extra-time goal to win the Club World Cup for the first time in their history.

Roberto Firmino provided the breakthrough on 98 minutes, with the Reds seeing a penalty award overturned by VAR during injury-time.

This is their first success having contested four finals across their history.
