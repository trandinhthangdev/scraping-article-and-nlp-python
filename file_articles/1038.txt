A four-year-old boy who cannot speak English was found today after he vanished from outside a church centre.

David Opoku went missing at the ABC Church Centre in Leeds at lunchtime.

West Yorkshire Police launched a frantic search to find the missing child.

David Opoku, 4, was last seen at the ABC Church Centre on Tempest road, Leeds, at lunchtime. West Yorkshire Police confirmed that the boy has now been found

The case was described as 'high risk' and urgent by West Yorkshire Police.

He was wearing a white long sleeved shirt, dark blue denim jeans & black shoes at the time of his disappearance.

The boy was found later this afternoon later safe and well.

Social media users hoped the boy was found, with one writing of 'that heart stopping dread, when you lose a child for a few minutes'
