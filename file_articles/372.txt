RIVERSIDE, Calif. (AP) - Ferron Flavors Jr. had 19 points, Milan Acquaah and Tre Armstrong scored 15 apiece, and Cal Baptist beat Mississippi Valley State 103-66 on Sunday night.

Glenn Morison added 13 points on 5-of-5 shooting and Reed Nottage scored 11 points for Cal Baptist.

The Lancers (8-4) scored the first seven points, opened a double-digit lead about 3 1/2 minutes into the game and used a 20-0 run to make it 32-7 with nine minutes left in the first half. Mississippi Valley State trailed by at least 20 the rest of the way.

Michael Green led the Delta Devils with 17 points and Caleb Hunter scored 11. Ahmadu Sarnor and Torico Simmons added 10 points apiece.

MVSU (0-12) has lost 14 consecutive games, dating to last season, the second-longest active streak of its kind in the nation.
