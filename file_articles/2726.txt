A man has been found dead in a Victorian park on Saturday morning.

The man's body was discovered in a public toilet block in the Belmont suburb at 7:30am.

A crime scene has been set up as police investigate to try and determine the circumstances of the suspicious death.

A man was found dead in a park in Belmont, in Victoria, at 7.30am Saturday (stock image)

The Geelong Advertiser reported that witnesses had seen a man on the ground near the public toilet block.

Both State Emergency Service and detectives attended the scene on Barrabool Road this morning.

Police are appealing for anyone with information or dash cam footage from the area at the time to contact Crime Stoppers on 1800 333 000.
