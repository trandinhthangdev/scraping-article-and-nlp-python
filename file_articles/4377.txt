SOFIA, Dec 20 (Reuters) - These are some of the main stories in Bulgarian newspapers on Friday. Reuters has not verified these stories and does not vouch for their accuracy.

-- Bulgaria plans to build new nuclear power assets and encourage households to use more biomass for heating as part of its efforts to meet EU green economy goals, the country's national energy and climate plan by 2030 showed. (Capital, 24 Chasa)

-- The share of thermal power plants, which currently produce about 45% of the country's electricity will drop to 30% by 2030 and 15% by 2035, the country's national energy and climate plan showed. (Capital, 24 Chasa)

- Four bidders have filed offers to supply state owned railway operator BDZ with 10 new locomotives in a 58 million levs tender. (24 Chasa, Monitor)
