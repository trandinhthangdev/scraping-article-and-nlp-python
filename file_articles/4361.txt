A five-year-old was rushed to hospital in critical condition after almost drowning at a home in north Sydney.

Emergency services received a call just before 5.30pm on Friday to an address on the Pacific Highway in Hornsby.

The boy was found in a pool at an apartment complex.

A NSW Ambulance spokesman told Daily Mail Australia that a five-year-old was taken to hospital.

The five-year-old child has been transported to Westmead Children's Hospital (pictured)

'The young child has been transported to Westmead Children’s Hospital,' he said.

'He remains in a critical condition.'
