Embarrassed about passing on an unwanted present? You shouldn’t be – nearly half of Britons will ‘regift’ something this Christmas.

A poll of 2,000 people found just under 50 per cent said they would rewrap at least one gift and pass it off as something new.

Among the most popular second-hand items are books, jewellery and handbags.

Craig Smith, of Mazuma Mobile, which carried out the poll, said: ‘There’s no longer a stigma attached to pre-loved items. More than a third of those surveyed said they would have an entirely pre-owned Christmas.’
