BUCHAREST, Dec 23 (Reuters) - Here are news stories, press reports and events to watch which may affect Romanian financial markets on Monday.

DEBT TENDER

Romanian debt managers tender 800 million lei ($186.04 million) worth of April 2026 treasury bonds.

GOVT MEETING

Romania's Liberal minority government holds a government meeting. The cabinet will fast-track the budget bill for 2020 and changes to a contested tax decree through parliament from 1400 GMT. Fast-tracking bills is faster than regular debates, but leaves the government open to a no confidence vote.

CEE MARKETS

Central European currencies were mixed on Friday in thin trade as investors began to wind down for the Christmas period.
