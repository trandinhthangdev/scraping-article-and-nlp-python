The search for a man who was swept off rocks into waters near Phillip Island on Victoria's coast will resume on Tuesday.

Fears are growing for the Hampton Park man, who has not been found after reportedly being swept off rocks by a large wave at mainland San Remo

It is believed he was fishing with several other men before the incident occurred shortly before midday on Monday, Victoria Police said.

There has been no update on the 41-year-old after emergency services swept the area off the coast near Phillip Island.

Water police and air wing units have combed the area where the man was last spotted but there have been no sightings, police said.
