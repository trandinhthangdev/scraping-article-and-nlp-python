A man has been killed in a crash on the Bruce Highway in central Queensland.

The 69-year-old man was driving near Ilbilbie, south of Mackay, around 2pm on Friday when his four-wheel drive crashed into a prime mover on the other side of the road.

The man was taken to Mackay Base Hospital where he later died. The driver of the prime mover was treated on scene for minor injuries.

Police are appealing for any motorists that may have seen the incident or have dashcam footage to contact them.
