A new case of measles was reported in Victoria on Monday, the latest of 57 cases recorded this year.

A visitor from Samoa was identified and the Department of Health said it is unrelated to previous cases notified in the state.

The most recent registered cases were infectious while attending public sites across metropolitan Melbourne and regional Victoria.

Six public exposure sites are currently listed for measles cases in Victoria, including Melbourne airport, a Virgin flight from Brisbane on November 30 and Shepparton's sports stadium.
