Manchester City host Leicester at Etihad Stadium in this evening's Premier League action.

Pep Guardiola's men will looking to narrow Brendan Rodgers lead over them by one point today following their 3-0 thrashing of Arsenal last weekend.

The Foxes will want to win today to keep their Premier League title hopes alive after a draw against Norwich put them 10 points behind Liverpool who they will face on Boxing Day.
