Police have launched a frantic hunt for a missing four-year-old boy who cannot speak English after he vanished from outside a church.

David Opoku was last seen at the ABC Church Centre in Leeds at lunchtime.

He is described as black with short curly black hair, and was last seen wearing a white long sleeved shirt, dark blue denim jeans and black shoes.

The boy was found later this afternoon later safe and well, reports West Yorkshire Police.
