A young man was rushed to hospital with stab wounds and two youths arrested after an all-in brawl broke out outside a Melbourne shopping centre.

More than 20 youths were involved in the brawl on Friday night, which sent the nearby Wyndham Vale Woolworths and BWS stores into lockdown.

When officers arrived dozens of the teenagers had already fled the scene.

A 21-year-old man was rushed to hospital with stab wounds and two teenagers charged with weapons offences after a violent brawl in Melbourne on Friday night

A 21-year-old Kurunjang man, who was stabbed in the brawl, was taken to hospital with non life-threatening injuries.

Police seized a taser and knife after the brawl.

A 19-year-old man was charged with possessing a controlled weapon and has been bailed to the Werribee Magistrates’ Court on 2 February 2020.

A 14-year-old boy has been charged with possessing a prohibited weapon and has been bailed to a children’s court at a later date.
