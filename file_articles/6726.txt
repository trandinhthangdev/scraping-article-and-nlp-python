-- China will actively support and prioritise non-subsidised solar power projects in 2020, state-backed Shanghai Securities News said, citing a draft notice of 2020 solar power projects issued by the National Energy Administration.

-- The notice, which will solicit opinions from provincial energy regulators before Dec. 31, did not mention the total subsidy amount or guiding electric prices for next year, the news outlet said.
