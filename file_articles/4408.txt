BUCHAREST, Dec 20 (Reuters) - Here are news stories, press reports and events to watch which may affect Romanian financial markets on Friday.

DEBT TENDERS

Romania's finance ministry sold a more than planned 1.0 billion lei ($233.25 million) worth of June 2024 bonds on Thursday at an average yield of 4.10%, but rejected all bids at a separate tender for one-year treasury bills, central bank data showed.

CEE MARKETS

Currencies in Central Eastern Europe were stuck in tight ranges on Thursday, with the Czech crown holding on to recent levels after the central bank sent a hawkish message to investors.
