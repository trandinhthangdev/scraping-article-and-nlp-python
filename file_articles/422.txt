A preschool aged boy was pulled unconscious from a backyard pool and rushed to hospital in critical condition.

An ambulance was called to a suburban home in Logan, Queensland at 2.46pm on Sunday where the young boy had been found unconscious in the water.

Family had pulled the boy out and given him CPR before emergency services arrived, a spokesperson from the Queensland Ambulance Service said.

A preschooler was rushed to the Queensland Children's Hospital in a critical condition after he was found unconscious in a backyard pool on Sunday (stock image)

The preschooler was then transported to the Queensland Children's Hospital in a critical condition.

On Monday morning he was in a critical but stable condition, according to the hospital's media representative.

The Queensland Children's Hospital spokesperson noted that the boy's family had asked for privacy and would not be providing any further updates.
